Acknowledgment - ripgrep (design inspiration; no obligation)
------------------------------------------------------------

Yagu's native search engine and its command-line output were designed with heavy
inspiration from ripgrep (https://github.com/BurntSushi/ripgrep) by Andrew
Gallant and contributors. Based on a direct comparison of Yagu's Rust source
against the ripgrep source, Yagu's scanner is an INDEPENDENT REIMPLEMENTATION: it
uses the published crates listed above (notably `regex`, `memchr`/`memmem`, and
`bstr`) as libraries and reproduces ripgrep's *ideas* - its scanning strategy,
the rare-byte literal prefilter (which originates in the `memchr` crate), and a
ripgrep-compatible output format - rather than copying ripgrep's source code.
No attribution is legally required for this (ideas and algorithms are not
copyrightable, and ripgrep is in any case available under the Unlicense); this
acknowledgment is provided purely as a courtesy. ripgrep is licensed
"Unlicense OR MIT".
