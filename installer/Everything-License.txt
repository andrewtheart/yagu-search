Everything — voidtools
======================

The "Offline" edition of Yagu (YaguSetup-<version>-x64-offline.exe) bundles the
official voidtools Everything setup (Everything-1.4.1.1032.x64-Setup.exe) so that
Everything can be installed on machines with no internet connection.

Everything is installed ONLY after you explicitly consent in Yagu. Yagu never
installs Everything silently — the bundled setup is run only when you accept the
in-app "Install Everything Search" prompt, and only after its Authenticode
signature is verified as a genuine voidtools binary.
